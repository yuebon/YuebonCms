About us

Company profile

Shanghai Yan Xuan precision mould Co., Ltd. is a professional design and manufacture of precision plastic mold production enterprises, is committed to automotive accessory products, computer peripheral products, household appliances and electronic products and other mold design and manufacturing.

Founded in 2011, The company has excellent technical team and advanced science and technology equipment, every product is produced with the strength of our team and the spirit of innovation, rigorous attitude, focus on excellence as the most perfect customer product design.

We will always adhere to the "quality first, customer first, strict reputation, serving the community" purpose, to contribute to the development of China's die and mold career.

Vision：:creating excellent brand of mould industry.
